The company claims to have already found three disease-causing genes, linking to a mental disease and two kinds of strokes.

As the rich get richer, the world has entered an “age of charity”, with education the most popular focus of some 260,000 foundations globally, researchers said recently.

The richest 1 percent of the world’s population owns half of its wealth, up from 43 percent in 2008, propelled in part by gains in financial assets, like stocks and bonds.
